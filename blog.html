Skill Development Program

December 2024 Update

This quarter, our skill development program launched three new training modules in digital marketing, solar panel installation, and organic farming. These additions respond to emerging market demands and sustainable livelihood opportunities.

Financial Literacy Initiative

December 2024 Update

We've introduced mobile financial literacy vans that travel to remote villages, making our financial education programs more accessible. The vans are equipped with multimedia content in local languages and interactive learning tools.
