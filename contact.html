Frequently Asked Questions

How can I volunteer with FluxxNova Foundation?

We welcome volunteers from all backgrounds! Visit our Get Involved page to learn about current opportunities, or contact us directly at volunteer@fluxxnova.org.

Where can I find your annual reports?

All our annual reports and financial documents are available on our Resources page for download. We believe in complete transparency.

How do you select communities for your programs?

We use a comprehensive needs assessment process that considers factors like poverty levels, access to services, community readiness, and potential for sustainable impact.

Can I visit your field programs?

Yes! We encourage visits from donors, partners, and supporters. Please contact our field offices to arrange a visit and see our programs in action.
