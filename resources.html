The Economic Impact of Women's Self-Help Groups

Research Paper | 45 pages | Published: November 2024

A comprehensive study analyzing the economic impact of women's self-help groups in rural areas, with findings from our five-year longitudinal study across 30 villages.

Key findings: 150% average income increase, 80% improvement in financial independence, significant positive impact on family welfare and children's education.

Analysis of the digital divide in rural education and practical recommendations for bridging the gap through innovative approaches and partnerships.
